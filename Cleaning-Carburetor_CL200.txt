CLEANING CARBURETOR

1. Fill ultrasonic cleaner with Simple Green Aircraft and Precision Cleaner (1 part cleaner to 3 parts water)

2. Put the large carburetor parts into the ultrasonic cleaner.

3. Put the small carburetor parts into one or more plastic bags and submerge the bag(s).

4. Set the heating to the max supported by your degreaser instructions.

5. Set the ultrasonic cleaner to run for 20 minutes. Repeat this at least two times.

6. Use a wire brush to clean the exterior of the carburetor of any stubborn residue if necessary.

7. Use thin gauge guitar string to clean various ports of the carburetor.

8. Run the carburetor through the ultrasonic cleaner for three 20 minute cycles.

9. Rinse the carburetor in water.

10. Dry the carburetor completely.

11. Proceed to reassembly.
